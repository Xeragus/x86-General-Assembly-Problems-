Write an assembly application following the instructions below:
- Insert 10 numbers (0-999) from keyboard and insert them into an array
- Search through the array and find the first occurence of number bigger than 500
- Revert the array from that position until the end of the array

Example:
The initial array:
323, 4, 284, 199, 45, 800, 67, 523, 79, 9
The reverted array:
323, 4, 284, 199, 45, 9, 79, 523, 67, 800
